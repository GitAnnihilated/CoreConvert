Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Welcome to CoreConvert. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CoreConvert collects, uses, and protects information when you use our file conversion services.

1. Information We Collect

CoreConvert may collect limited information necessary to operate the service, including:

  • Files uploaded for conversion
  • Basic technical data such as browser type, device type, and IP address
  • Usage data such as pages visited and conversion requests

We do not collect sensitive personal information unless it is voluntarily provided by the user.

2. File Processing

Files uploaded to CoreConvert are processed solely for the purpose of converting them to the requested format.

  • Uploaded files are temporarily stored during processing.
  • Converted files are made available for download.
  • Files are automatically deleted from our servers after a short retention period.

We do not access, view, or share file contents except as necessary to perform the conversion.

3. Use of Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the conversion service
  • Improve system performance and reliability
  • Monitor usage for security and fraud prevention
  • Analyze website usage trends

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect user data during file upload, conversion, and download.

However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

CoreConvert may use third-party tools or hosting providers to operate the platform. These providers may process technical data required for service delivery.

6. Children's Privacy

CoreConvert does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the website contact page.
